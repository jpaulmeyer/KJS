What We Do

Repartus is a CS179 Project by Kahu Foster, Shantell Williams, and J.Paul Meyer

We spoke to many people that have been seeking work for over 6 months. These people are hardworking and highly skilled. However, they often face challenges getting callbacks for jobs. Many shared with us that they struggle to effectively showcase their experiences. We believe these individuals have the experiences to be successful in many roles but could use some help higlighting their past experiences and added value. Our project seeks to help candidates present themselves in the best way and increase the likelihood they will be interviewed for future roles.
